# TalkTime

A free, anonymous social + private messaging app. No account, no sign‑up, no
email — just open it and go. **Everyone is anonymous.**

## 📱 Open it on your iPhone

### 👉 https://gamer4life36.github.io/talktime-web/

Add it to your Home Screen so it opens fullscreen, just like a normal app:

1. Open the link above in **Safari**.
2. Tap **Share** (the square with the up arrow).
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**.
4. Launch **TalkTime** from your Home Screen.

That's it — **no App Store, no download, no account.** Just open and go.

*(Also works in any modern mobile browser.)*
